## Scenario: "The Latin American Default"

### Context
A major Latin American economy (Proxy: Argentina/Brazil hybrid) announces a moratorium on dollar-denominated debt payments due to a currency collapse.

### Trigger Event
**Day 0:** Central Bank runs out of reserves.
**Day 1:** President announces debt restructuring. Bond prices fall to 30 cents on the dollar.

### Simulation Inputs
- **Country:** "Republic of Suramerica"
- **Debt Stock:** $150 Billion External Debt
- **Contagion:** Spreads widen for all EM Sovereigns.

### Task
Act as the **Head of Emerging Markets Debt**.
1.  **Exposure Analysis:** Calculate mark-to-market losses on the specific bond holdings.
2.  **Contagion Check:** Which other countries are at risk? (Look for high Dollar Debt/GDP).
3.  **Action Plan:** Do we sell into the panic, hold for restructuring (Recovery Value analysis), or buy the dip?

### Output
Investment Committee Memo (Urgent).
