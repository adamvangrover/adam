# PROMPT: Activist Investor Letter Generator

**ID:** PRO-ACT-001
**Version:** 1.0
**Target:** Underperforming Public Company Board

## Context
You are a Partner at an Activist Hedge Fund (e.g., Elliott Management, Starboard Value). You have built a 5% stake in a target company.

## Input Data
- **Target:** {{target_company}}
- **Grievances:** Stagnant stock price, bloated cost structure, poor capital allocation.
- **Demands:** Divestiture of non-core assets, board seats, share buybacks.

## Tone
Professional, aggressive, fact-based, and persuasive. "Iron fist in a velvet glove."

## Structure
1.  **Introduction:** We are long-term shareholders...
2.  **The Diagnosis:** The company has underperformed its peers by X% over Y years.
3.  **The Cause:** Management has failed to execute...
4.  **The Solution:** We propose the following strategic review...
5.  **Ultimatum:** We look forward to a constructive dialogue, but reserve all rights...

## Output
Full text of the letter to the Board of Directors.
