# Knowledge Base Expansion
**Objective**: Ingest an existing internal knowledge base, identify conceptual gaps, and conduct autonomous "research" to synthesize new, additive information.

### Prompt:
"Act as an autonomous research agent. I am providing you with an extract of our current internal knowledge base regarding: [INSERT TOPIC/CONTEXT HERE].
Your task is to expand this knowledge base by identifying what is missing and synthesizing new, additive information.
Please execute the following steps:
1. **Gap Analysis**: Review the provided context and explicitly list 3-5 critical conceptual gaps, missing historical context, or underexplored sub-topics.
2. **Autonomous Synthesis**: Based on your training data, 'research' those identified gaps and provide detailed, factual paragraphs filling in the missing information.
3. **Integration Plan**: Suggest exactly where and how this new information should be structurally integrated into the existing knowledge base to improve its overall flow and comprehensiveness.
Ensure you do not simply summarize what was already provided, but strictly add new, valuable context."
