# Historical Evolution
**Objective**: Trace the timeline and historical development of a concept.

### Prompt:
"Act as a historian of science and ideas. Your task is to trace the historical evolution and timeline of the following concept: [INSERT TOPIC HERE].
Your response must include:
1. **Origins & Early Thought**: Where and when the concept first emerged.
2. **Key Milestones & Breakthroughs**: A chronological outline of major discoveries, paradigm shifts, or influential figures that advanced the concept.
3. **Modern Synthesis**: How the concept is understood today compared to its origins.
4. **Future Trajectory**: Current frontiers or expected future developments related to this topic.
Focus on factual accuracy and chronological coherence."
