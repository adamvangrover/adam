General Guidance

As you use the prompt library, keep these key principles in mind to ensure the highest quality analysis.

  • ✓

    Be Specific

    Always clearly state the company name and the time periods you are analyzing.

  • ✓

    Provide Rationale

    Don't just state a conclusion. Clearly link your analysis and data to your recommendations.

  • ✓

    Define Your Terms

    Ensure financial metrics are clearly defined and calculated consistently.

  • ✓

    Know Your Context

    Tailor your analysis to the specific request—is it a new loan, an annual review, or an event-driven update?

  • ✓

    Break It Down

    For very complex companies or situations, use multiple, focused prompts to build your analysis piece by piece.
