Market Mayhem (Executive Summary)

Market sentiment is recovering as inflation shows signs of moderating and corporate earnings remain strong. The S&P 500 is up 0.8% for the week, while the Nasdaq has gained 1.2%. However, geopolitical risks persist, and the upcoming Federal Reserve meeting will be closely watched for signals on the future direction of monetary policy.
