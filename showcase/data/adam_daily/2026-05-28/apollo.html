Data Synthesis

Instead of static prices, marks shift daily based on changing interest rates, credit spreads, and actual market trends. Apollo observes live trades and baselines against comparable public valuations.

Strategic Partnerships

Apollo is collaborating closely with Intercontinental Exchange Inc. (ICE) via ICE Private Credit Intelligence. This partnership provides the external data infrastructure needed to optimize data tracking.

🛡️

Conservative Alignment

To ensure trust and mitigate risk, for positions shared with third parties, Apollo plans a conservative approach: aligning its marks to the lowest observable valuation available.

👥

Retail Demand

Individual and wealth-channel investors are increasingly moving into private assets. Unlike institutional capital, these cohorts require liquidity-like visibility and daily confidence in their holdings.

📉

Redemption Jitters

The industry is facing pressure from record retail redemption requests. Increased portfolio transparency is a direct mechanism to calm these jitters and build investor confidence.
