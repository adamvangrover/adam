The T+1 Settlement Transition

Executive Summary & Institutional Context on the accelerated settlement cycle mechanics.

Mechanics of the Shift

The transition from T+2 to T+1 settlement halves the time market participants have to execute post-trade processing, allocate, confirm, and affirm trades.

  • Liquidity Demand: Increased need for intraday liquidity to meet tighter funding windows.
  • FX Misalignment: Mismatch between T+1 securities settlement and T+2 standard FX settlement.
  • Securities Lending: Compressed timeline for recalls, increasing risk of fails.
